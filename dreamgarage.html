Dream Garage — Site Plan

Simple plan for a three-page car enthusiast website (home / catalog / favorites).

Site Name & Reason

Dream Garage — chosen because it communicates a curated collection of desirable cars (modern, classic, and performance) and fits the theme of a showcase site.

Site Purpose

Provide a compact, browsable showcase of cars with specs, images, and the ability for visitors to mark favorites. The catalog will be generated from a local JSON file and include details and images for each car.

Visitor Scenarios

  • What are the specs (year, engine, horsepower, drivetrain) for this model?
  • How can I save a short list of favorite cars to review later?

Color Scheme

Two primary colors used across the site for a consistent brand:

Primary
#0b3d91
Accent
#ff7a18

Usage: Primary for headings, navigation, card accents; Accent for CTAs, buttons, badges.

Typography

Headings — Montserrat (weights 600/700)
Body — Roboto (weights 300/400/500)

Two web fonts keep the design clean: Montserrat for brand headings and Roboto for readable body text.

Wireframe — Home Page (mobile & desktop)

Header / Brand
Hero image (short intro)
Quick featured car (card)
Catalog preview (list of car tiles)
Footer / links
Header / navSearch / favorites icon
Left: Hero / Featured car — Right: Quick stats or CTA
Catalog grid (3 columns) — car cards with image + short specs
Modal (detail) triggered from cards
Footer with attributions & video link

Notes: catalog page will display at least 15 cars. Each car card shows model, year, engine, horsepower, and thumbnail. Clicking a card opens an accessible modal with more details and an add/remove favorite button (persisted to localStorage).

Content & Assets

  • Local JSON file (cars.json) with 15+ car objects and 6–8 properties each.
  • Optimized JPEG/WebP thumbnails (lazy-loaded) using intrinsic aspect ratios.
  • Attributions page in footer for image sources.

Testing & Self-Check

Planned checks before submission:

  • Validate HTML semantics (header, nav, main, footer).
  • Responsive check at 320px (mobile) and desktop widths — no horizontal scroll.
  • Fetch (local JSON) with try...catch and graceful error message UI.
  • Confirm localStorage favorites persist on reload.
  • Accessible modal: focus trap, keyboard close (Esc), and ARIA labels.
  • Page weight checks: images optimized and lazy-loaded to keep pages under 500kB.
  • Meta tags present: title, description, author, and basic Open Graph tags.
